## Welcome to Brand Identity

You've defined your brand strategy and positioning in Module 6. Now it's time to bring your brand to life visually. This module covers the visual elements that make your brand recognizable, memorable, and emotionally resonant.

Great brand identity isn't about pretty pictures. It's about visual systems that communicate your archetype, reinforce your positioning, and create instant recognition. From your logo to your color palette to your design wireframes, every visual choice should tell your brand story.

## Why This Module Matters

In the job market, your visual identity is often the first impression you make. A well-designed portfolio site, a professional logo, and a cohesive visual system signal that you:

1. **Understand design thinking** even if you're not a designer
2. **Take your personal brand seriously** and pay attention to details
3. **Can create compelling presentations** of your work
4. **Align visual choices with strategic thinking** from archetype to execution

The Madison Framework projects you build in Modules 1-5 need to be presented beautifully. This module gives you the visual toolkit to do exactly that.
